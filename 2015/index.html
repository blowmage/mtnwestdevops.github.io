Still DevOps Presentations!

While MountainWest DevOps isn't happening in 2015, MountainWest RubyConf and MountainWest JavaScript are still very much looking for DevOps presentations. Even before having a dedicated day for DevOps, MountainWest has a long history of highlighting operations and production focused speakers. If that is you, please consider submitting a proposal to one or both of those conferences.
